Load a matrix of characters which represents city map.
Characters can be :
	X is building/wall
	. is part of a road/path
	- is person
	0 is sandwich
Then calculate maximum amount of sandwiches that person can eat and distance from starting position to the most distant sandwich.
Calculate how many steps did journey for sandwiches take.
Person can move in 4 standard directions (up, down, left, right).
